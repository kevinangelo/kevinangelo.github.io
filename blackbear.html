Why I chose UMaine...

1) Price
2) Academics
3)Beautiful campus (as shown in video)
4)Great Environment
5) School Pride

My first semester was not what I expected when I came to the University of Maine. However, this suprise happened to be one that turned out for the better. I felt like it was going to be a challenge with all the classes that I was taking and also the challenge of making new friends. I was pleasantly suprised when I found out that everyone was in the same boat as me! Every person I met was willing to befriend me because they were in the same situation as me. Classes werent nearly as difficult as I thought they would be and it looks like I'll finish this semester with a higher GPA than I ever had in high school. I realized that you can have fun and do well in academics at the same time! I cant what the University of Maine has in-store for me over the next 3 1/2 years!
